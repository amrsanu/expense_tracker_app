Create app password for Gmail

Steps to Create App password

  1. Log into your Gmail account.
  2. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the Gmail interface and then click on the “My Account” button.
  3. On the “My Account” page, click on the “Sign-in & security” tab.
  4. Scroll down to the “App passwords” section and click “Generate”.
  5. Select the app you want to create a password for and click “Generate”.
  6. You will then be presented with your app password. Make sure to copy the password and store it in a secure location.
  7. Use the app password to log in to the app.
